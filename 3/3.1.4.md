Consider the system of linear equations
$\begin{bmatrix}
   6 & -3 \\
   2 & 6
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
   x_1  \\
   x_2 
\end{bmatrix}=
\begin{bmatrix}
   3  \\
   1 
\end{bmatrix}$
1. Solve the system in $\mathbb{F}_p$ when $p=5$, $11$, and $17$. 
2. Determine the number of solutions when $p=7$.
