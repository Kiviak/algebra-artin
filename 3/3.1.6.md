Solve completely the systems of linear equations $AX=0$ and $AX=B$, where
$A=\begin{bmatrix}
   1 & 1 & 0\\
   1 & 0 & 1 \\
   1 & -1 & -1
\end{bmatrix},
B=\begin{bmatrix}
   1   \\
   -1  \\
   1
\end{bmatrix}$ 
1. in $\mathbb{Q}$.
2. in $\mathbb{F}_2$. 
3. in $\mathbb{F}_3$. 
4. in $\mathbb{F}_7$.
