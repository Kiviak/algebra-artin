- if $c\in F$ and $v_1\in V^{(\lambda)}$, then $T(cv_1)=cT(v_1)=c\lambda v_1=\lambda(cv_1)$, then $cv_1\in V^{(\lambda)}$.  

thus, $V^{(\lambda)}$ is a subspace of $V$.
