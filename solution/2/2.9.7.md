since 
$A=\begin{bmatrix}
   1 & 1 \\
   0 & 1
\end{bmatrix},
A^2=\begin{bmatrix}
   1 & 2 \\
   0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}, 
 A^3=\begin{bmatrix}
   1 & 0 \\
   0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}$
we have the order of $A$ is $3$.
