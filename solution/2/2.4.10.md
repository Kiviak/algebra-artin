Consider $GL_2(\R)$, and the following matrices in $GL_2(\R)$:
$A=\begin{bmatrix}
   -1 & 1 \\
   0 & 1
\end{bmatrix},
B=\begin{bmatrix}
   -1 & 0 \\
   0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}$
then 
$A^2=\begin{bmatrix}
   1 & 0 \\
   0 & 1
\end{bmatrix},
B^2=\begin{bmatrix}
   1 & 0 \\
   0 & 1
\end{bmatrix},
AB=\begin{bmatrix}
   1 & 1 \\
   0 & 1
\end{bmatrix},
(AB)^n=\begin{bmatrix}
   1 & n \\
   0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}$
it is clear $A,B$ have finite order and $AB$ has infinite order.
